You don't want an HOA

You really don't want an HOA. Repeat that with me 3 times. You don't want an HOA.

I'm writing this mostly for myself as a record of just how bad it is to have an HOA, in the hopes that in the future if I ever consider having an HOA again, I can talk myself out of it immediately. If it helps other people, great. But I think there's plenty of literature out there on this topic already. I knew of it, but nothing too specific, and now that I have some experience, it's too valuable not to write down.

HOAs do things like send you annoying notices for a cardboard box on your front porch. The infuriating thing is that someone somewhere is spending their time and money doing this. They saw your house, they filed a notice, they want you to upload proof that it's taken care of, and if not, they will fine you.

Meanwhile, we have things like a fire sprinkler riser alarm going off for months outside the units, and nothing has happened.

HOAs do things like tow people's cars because they park outside for a night or two (perhaps their garage door is broken, read on for more).

Meanwhile, think of the audacity of residents to request the HOA to remove an un-registered, clearly abandoned car that everyone wants gone. The police say they can't do anything because the car is on private HOA property. The HOA sits for almost a year and finally remove it, then blames the abandoned car on some random resident, some poor sap whose address I don't know how they got, probably chosen randomly from a hat, and that resident has to defend themselves (guilty until proven innocent, anyone?) for some stupid reason.

HOAs do things like start towing people's cars 20 minutes after they park in the community, where there are always dozens of empty parking spots. They claim to be protecting the "security" and parking spaces for people.

Meanwhile, our HOA is aware that people are stealing our mail using a master key, and can't do anything about it for years, even though we know exactly which cars, when they come, and have the people on video.
