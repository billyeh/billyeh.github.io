If you're asking me how I am via text message, I have to assume one of two things:

  1. You're trying to actually know how I am.
  2. You're just asking me that question as a preface to another, actual request or question.

Of course, there's a third option, which is so illogical that there's no way it would make any sense whatsoever. You could be asking how I am, kind of like how Americans tend to do that in person, where the de facto answer is "Good!" or "Doing well." I hope no one ever does this because texting is already such a bad way to communicate.
